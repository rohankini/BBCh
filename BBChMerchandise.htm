BBCh Merchandise

BBCh T-Shirt

T-shirt Description:
- BBCH t-shirts have come to life on demand by our cycling community who has liked the exclusive designs. We are happy to initiate this process of making the adorable t-shirts available for everyone.
- Being in the tropical country, it is most comfortable to wear complete cotton t-shirt almost for any occasion trouble-free.
- The fabric is smooth and ideal to wrap around the skin.

Sizing Chart:
Shipping Description:

SHIPPING WITHIN BANGALORE: FREE
if you are ordering online within Bangalore then relax and wait at home to receive your order by speed post or courier within few working days and the shipping cost is on us.

SHIPPING WITHIN INDIA: 30 RUPEES
Friends who are ordering from different parts of India, outside Bangalore, will receive it by speed post or courier in few days’ time at a nominal cost of ₹30 only.

NO INTERNATIONAL SHIPPING
We regret not to have facility of shipping overseas but you never know, may be someday !

Return & Exchanges Possibility:
We really do not foresee this but in case of requirement to return or exchange, you may please do the following.

EXCHANGES Let's keep it simple. If the delivered t-shirt does not fit on you or if you find any manufacturing defect then immediately please email us at council@bbch.in and let us know your order number and the size you wish to exchange for. Kindly return your t-shirt in an un-worn condition and we will send you your preferred one by speed post ir courier within a reasonable time and we will notify you via email once your return has been processed. We would request you to track your parcel through tracking number.

Kindly note the Return address for exchanges:
Bangalore Bicycle Championship
C/o, Promise V Gangani
#212, 2nd floor, 8th Main, 9th Cross, Byarasandra,
Jayanagar 1st Block East,
Bangalore - 560011
India
M - +91 9886046777

If we are sold out on your ordered item then we will offer you a FULL refund of the purchase price. Please note that the shipping charge for the replacement product will be ₹ 30 for domestic shipments in case of sizing issues. In case of manufacturing defects, we shall bear the two way shipping charges that will be involved in the complete exchange process. Apart from any other reason mentioned earlier, we will not be in a position to entertain any refund.
