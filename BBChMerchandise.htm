Now no more stuck in rain and No more wait for that perfect weather to step out, cover your backpack with BBCH Rain Cover for backpack bag and rest assured about your belongings in bag, this Specially Waterproof rain cover keep your backpack and belongings dry from rain, mud and dust.

Now riders/biker don't have to worry about dislodge of their bag Raincover, BBCH raincover for backpack is specially designed and Velcro tape holds the bag tight, No more dislodge in heavy wind for bikers.

Material: Specially reinforced dual PU coated Waterproof Material
Compatibility: For medium sized backpacks upto 25-35 Ltr

- BBCh logo / Cycle logo on cover with reflective tape

- Waterproof Material
- Separate Pouch to keep wet Rain cover
- Reflective tapes for better visibility in night.
