BBCh14 Race #01 - Individual Time Trial

Go Back

When : Sunday, 23 February 2014
Where : Emerald Isle Resort | Old Madras Road
Reporting Time : 6:15AM
Race Start : 7:00AM
Distance : 33.5kms Only
Race Fee : Online -INR 300/- , Spot - 400/- (Race free for Students – flash your ID Card!)

Register To Race |  Volunteer Signup

The 2014 season of the awesome Bangalore Bicycle Championships is going to kick off with a 33.5km Individual Time Trial on Old Madras Road.

The start point is Emerald Isle Resort on Old Madras Road in Hoskote (same location as the Team Time Trial race last year).

Get your race face on people and get ready for a rocking new season at the Bangalore Bicycle Championships.

The Route :
Out and back along the Old Madras Road, starting in the service road, entering the highway immediately. Stay on the highway for approximately 16.5km, and take a U-turn just after the Confident Amoon Resort. You’ll experience headwinds on the way out, so fight through it and enjoy the return!

How to Get There :
The start point is situated on the service road adjacent to Emerald Isle Resort, approximately 6km past the toll gates on the Old Madras Road, heading towards Kolar.

Map to race start : http://bit.ly/emerald-isle-map
