BBCh15 Race #05 - KIADB IT Park Circuit Race

Go Back

When : Sunday, 21st June 2015
Where : KIADB IT Park | Budigere Main Road
Lap Distance : 11.5kms Circuit (as per Car odo & will vary a bit as per Garmin)
Reporting Time : 6:15AM
Race Start : 7:00AM (Category Wise)
Race Fee : Online -INR 300/- NO Spot Registration (Race free for Kids & U-18 Category – flash your ID Card!, "No ID Card No U18 BIB")

Register To Race |  Volunteer Signup
Note: Registration will be closed by Friday, 19th (midnight)

The 2015 season of the awesome Bangalore Bicycle Championships is going with Circuit Race at KIADB IT Park at Singanahalli Village, On Budigere Main Road.

Get your race face on people and get ready for a rocking at the Bangalore Bicycle Championships.

Map to KIADB IT Park :
KIADB IT Park
Gollahalli, Singanahalli Village,
Budigere Main Road,
Bangalore, Karnataka
India.
Google Map : https://goo.gl/maps/gvpO2

YouTube video of the course : Circuit Loop Video

PRE-RACE DETAILS
The Race will start at 7:00am. Get to the venue at 6-6:15am. Make sure you pre-register and digitally sign your indemnity form online, so you just have to collect your bib numbers on race day. The start will be KIADB IT Park at Singanahalli Village, On Budigere Main Road.

Race Details
- 11.5km : Circuit distance (as per Car odo & will vary a bit as per Garmin)

- Elite & Masters : 6 Laps | Start : 7 O'Clock (9:30 Cutoff time)
- Amatuer : 4 Laps | Start : 7:10 O'Clock (9:10 Cutoff time)
- Women & U-18 : 3 Laps | Start : 7:20 O'Clock (8:50 Cutoff time)
- Kids : 1 Small Lap | Start : 8 O'Clock


The Route :

Here’s the route for the KIADB IT Park Circuit Road Race.

Finish Line

@ 9:50 O'Clock - Podium
Race Notes :
  • It’s a pretty even and smooth road surface.
  • Water and refreshments will be available at the venue.
  • Riders are expected to be on self supported race mode including mechanical fixing, flat fixing, carrying their own toolkit, towing of the cycle if required etc.
  • Emergency ambulance support will be available (Thanks to Sita Bhateja speciality hospital and Spectrum Physio. You guys are awesome!)
  • Any rider can contact the race director through a specified BBCh phone number which will be active ONLY on race day (+91 74069 98925)
  • Riders are expected to read and understand and adhere to the rules of the race available on the website http://bbch.in/RulesBook.htm
  • The race referee reserves the sole discretion of analyzing any situation during the race and his opinion will be considered final.
  • Registration is strictly on-line https://in.explara.com/beta/bbch15-race5 and no spot registration will be allowed
  • If you would like to get in touch with the the core team, please do shoot us an email at council@bbch.in
