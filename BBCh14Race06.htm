BBCh14 Race #06 - Gunjur Lake Circuit Race (102 km)

Go Back

When : Sunday, 20 July 2014
Where : Nandi Base | Gunjur Lake
Reporting Time : 6:15AM
Race Start : 7:30AM
Distance : 102km Only
Race Cut Off Time : 4:00 Hours
Race Fee : Online -INR 300/- (NO Spot Registration (Race free for Students – flash your ID Card!)

Register To Race |  Volunteer Signup

Note: Registration will be closed by Friday, 18th (midnight)

The 2014 season of the awesome Bangalore Bicycle Championships is going with a Nandi base, Gunjur Lake Circuit Race 102km. The Circuit is set in the Nandi Valley, with the imposing rock face of Makalidurga Hill to the west and Gunjur Lake to the East.

Get your race face on people and get ready for a rocking at the Bangalore Bicycle Championships.

PRE-RACE DETAILS
The Race will start at 7:30am. Get to the venue at 6-6:15am. Make sure you pre-register and digitally sign your indemnity form online, so you just have to collect your bib numbers on race day. The start will be Base of Nandi Climb (a.k.a. the "Nandi" Bull statue).

Race Details
- 18 km : Neutral rollout
- 28km : Circuit distance
- 84 km : Race distance
- 102km : Total distance
- 4hrs : Cut-off time!
- 200m : Elevation gain/loop
- 3 : Number of out and back loops

Circuit Description : 18 - 102km: Gunjur Lake Circuit Race
- Race "Kilometer 0" is at the 18 km mark
- This is where the racing starts after the 18km neutral rollout
- The Race does 3 laps of 28km Gunjur Lake Circuit for a total of 84 km of racing

Race Route Description
- The Circuit is set in the Nandi Valley, with the imposing rock face of Makalidurga Hill to the west and Gunjur Lake to the East.
- Each loop drops and regains 200m per lap and the fun is having to do it thrice!
- Challenges include the race distance, the exposed nature of the course, cross head winds and the repeated elevation loss/gain!

RIDE TIPS
  • Try and get a long ride (or two) around the 3hr+ mark in this weekend
  • It will take anywhere from 2:45 to 4:30hrs to complete this distance
  • Drink: 600ml/hr, so that’s a minimum of 2-3 bottles
  • Eat: 200-300 Calories/hr (800-1200 kJ/hr)
  • Replenish electrolytes lost in sweat through Gatorade, Enerzal or Electral
  • The human body can, in theory, go forever, so everyone can finish this distance, provided you fuel it right
  • A banana and a bottle of water is NOT going to cut it!

RACE ROUTE DETAILS :
0 - 18km: "Neutral Rollout"
- Narrow roads undergoing widening work means we will not race this section
- A lead moto will set an even 25kmph pace up to race "Kilometer 0"
- Riders are not allowed to be pass the lead moto in the neutral rollout

18 - 102km: Gunjur Lake Circuit Race
- Race "Kilometer 0" is at the 18 km mark
- This is where the racing starts after the 18km neutral rollout
- The race does 3 laps of 28km Gunjur Lake Circuit for a total of 84 km of racing

- Each loop drops and regains 200m per lap and the fun is having to do it thrice!
- Challenges include the race distance, the exposed nature of the course, cross head winds and the repeated elevation loss/gain!

Race Notes :
  • Water and refreshments will be available at the all U-turn.
  • Riders are expected to be on self supported race mode including mechanical fixing, flat fixing, carrying their own toolkit, towing of the cycle if required etc.
  • Emergency ambulance support will be available (Thanks to Sita Bhateja speciality hospital and Spectrum Physio. You guys are awesome!)
  • Any rider can contact the race director through a specified BBCh phone number which will be active ONLY on race day (+91 74069 98925)
  • Riders are expected to read and understand and adhere to the rules of the race available on the website http://bbch.in/RulesBook.htm
  • The race referee reserves the sole discretion of analyzing any situation during the race and his opinion will be considered final.
  • Registration is strictly on-line http://bbch14-race6.doattend.com and no spot registration will be allowed
  • If you would like to get in touch with the the core team, please do shoot us an email at council@bbch.in
