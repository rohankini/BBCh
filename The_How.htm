How does it all work?

Ok peeps, we have taken all your suggestions into consideration and have tweaked things considerably. Please refer to the updated Rules and Regulations which outline the points system we will be following this year. Points assigned in a category will now be based on the number of riders racing in that category.

As such, this is pretty final for this year but we’re always happy to hear your thoughts on the subject so, if you have any, please do write to us at council@bbch.in.
