BBCh16 Race #08 - Team Time Trial

Go Back


When : Sunday, 17 September 2017
Where : Emerald Isle Resort | Old Madras Road
Reporting Time : 6:00AM
Race Start : 7:00AM
Distance : 49.4kms Only
Race Cut Off Time : 130 Minutes

Race Fee : Online -INR 500/- (e.g - a team - 3 pays Rs 500x3 = Rs. 1,500), NO Spot Registration

Note: Team may be made up of a MINIMUM OF TWO & MAXIMUM OF THREE Riders at the start of the Race.

Register To Race |  Volunteer Signup
FB Event : https://www.facebook.com/events/654074701432665/

Note: Registration will be closed by Wednesday, 13th (Midnight)

The 2017 season of the awesome Bangalore Bicycle Championships is going with a 49.4km Team Time Trial on Old Madras Road.
The start point is Emerald Isle Resort on Old Madras Road in Hoskote.
Get your race face on people and get ready for a rocking at the Bangalore Bicycle Championships.

The Route :
Out and back along the Old Madras Road, starting in the service road, entering the highway immediately. Stay on the highway for approximately 24.7km, and take a U-turn just after oneway flyover.

How to Get There :
The start point is situated on the main road, next to Emerald Isle Resort, approximately 10km past the toll gates on the Old Madras Road, heading towards Kolar.

Map to race start : http://bit.ly/emerald-isle-map

49.4Km TTT Route : https://goo.gl/maps/hD2AX6U8f882

PRE-RACE DETAILS
The race will start at 7:00 AM as per categories and Team start time. Get to the venue before 30 minutes before your Team Start Time. Make sure your team pre-register and digitally sign your indemnity form online, so you just have to collect your bib numbers on race day. The start/finish will be at Emerald Isle Resort.




Team Formation:
Teams of Minimum 2 riders and Maximum 3 for all categories

Categories:
Elite: (Any combination of riders from Elite / Masters / Amateur / U-18 / Women is allowed to form a team in Elite category)

Amateur: (Any combination of riders from Amateur / Masters / U-18 / Women is allowed to form a team in Amateur category)

Masters(40+) : (All riders need to be above 40 years to qualify for this category)

Women: (All riders in the team need to be female to qualify for this category)

U-18: (All riders in the team need to be U-18 to fall in this category)



Categories :
* Elite (Open for all)
 PS : Who participate in Elite should stick to Elite category for rest of the season, they cann't go back to Amateur in other road races of this season.
Elite category is also open for anybody who wants to race but since that is the category where most of the pro cyclists take part, it is better to have some good level of experience to enroll into Elite category otherwise it might cause inconvenience for the entire peloton due to handling and skill set.
* Amateur
 Amateur category is open for anybody who wants to race irrespective of age.
* Master 40+ (Open for Men and Women)
 Participants age should be above 40 years as on race day. Riders are free to participate in Elite/Amateur category if they wish to.
Masters category is for anybody who is above 40 years of age irrespective of gender.
* Women
 - Girls below the age of 18 may opt for Women category also if they wish to.
* Under18 (both boys and girls)
 Participants age should lesser than 18 years as on race day. Proof of age needs to be produced at the time of bib collection
Under-18 category is common for both boys and girls who are below the age of 18.





Race Details:
Race Venue / Parking / Registration Desk : Emerald Isle Resort, approximately 10km past the toll gates on the Old Madras Road.
Start Point : Opp. to Emerald Isle Resort.
Points of caution from start-point :
@ 7km Busy Intersection to watch out.
@ 22km Fly-over approaching to a small hillock.
@ 23km Left side Cafe Coffee Day outlet with HP petrol bunk.

@ 24.7km U- turn, with BBCh sign marked on road.
(24.7km as per car odometer and may vary little bit as per cyclo-comp or Garmin)
    
@ 41km Busy Intersection to watch out.

@ 49.4km - Finish Line

@ 10:30 O'Clock - Podium
Team Time Trial: Rules & Regulations :
Teams may be made up of a minimum of two riders and a maximum of three riders at the start of the race.

Team times will be based on the following;
a. If 3 riders start, the time will be based on the second rider at the finish
b. If 2 riders start, the time will be based on the second rider at the finish

Elite, Amateur, Masters, Women, and under-18 teams must include only riders from those individual categories.

There will be no championship points earned by the individual riders. Teams with more than THREE riders can submit a second (and third) team to race the TTT.

More on the points system, rider conduct, support vehicles etc are all explained in the awesome BBCh race rule book – http://bbch.in/RulesBook.htm
Race Notes :
  • It’s a pretty even and smooth road surface which is mildly rolling.
  • We will have a volunteer at the U-turn point. Water and refreshments will be available at the start and finish line.
  • Riders are expected to be on self supported race mode including mechanical fixing, flat fixing, carrying their own toolkit, towing of the cycle if required etc.
  • Emergency ambulance support will be available (Thanks to Sita Bhateja speciality hospital and Spectrum Physio. You guys are awesome!)
  • Any rider can contact the race director through a specified BBCh phone number which will be active ONLY on race day (+91 74069 98925)
  • Riders are expected to read and understand and adhere to the rules of the race available on the website http://bbch.in/RulesBook.htm
  • The race referee reserves the sole discretion of analyzing any situation during the race and his opinion will be considered final
  • Registration is strictly on-line https://in.explara.com/e/bbch17-race08 and no spot registration will be allowed
  • If you would like to get in touch with the the core team, please do shoot us an email at council@bbch.in
