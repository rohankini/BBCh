The 2015 season of the awesome Bangalore Bicycle Championships is going with a First MTB Dirt Race of the season at Nandi hills road (on your right side), just before the Prestige Golfshire.

Get your race face on people and get ready for a rocking at the Bangalore Bicycle Championships.

RACE DETAILS
The Route :
Here’s the route for the MTB Dirt 4.4Kms Road Race.
https://www.strava.com/activities/347889671




Race Details:
Race Venue / Parking / Registration Desk : Just before Prestige Golfshire on Nandi hills road, on your right side - 4 km from Nandi Cross, Bangalore-Hyderabad Hwy
Start Point : Thimmaraya Swamy Hill
Points of caution from start-point :

1st Part :
The first 700 mtrs are off road climb, it’s a very steep climb with some loose rocks. The climb is followed by a downhill of around 500 mtrs.
2nd Part :
This part is almost flat section with single/jeep track including a lake bund. After lake bund, there is a tarmac road for 300 mtrs.
3rd Part :
This section is technical single track for 1.1km, it include climbing & descending on rocks, negotiating trees, loose rock climb. Riders need to be careful in this section.
4th Part :
Flat off road for sprint finish
@ 10:00 O'Clock - Podium :
Race Notes :
  • It’s a MTB Dirt Race so ride with safety gears.
  • Water and refreshments will be available at the venue.
  • Riders are expected to be on self supported race mode including mechanical fixing, flat fixing, carrying their own toolkit, towing of the cycle if required etc.
  • Emergency ambulance support will be available (Thanks to Sita Bhateja speciality hospital and Spectrum Physio. You guys are awesome!)
  • Any rider can contact the race director through a specified BBCh phone number which will be active ONLY on race day (+91 74069 98925)
  • Riders are expected to read and understand and adhere to the rules of the race available on the website http://bbch.in/RulesBook.htm
  • The race referee reserves the sole discretion of analyzing any situation during the race and his opinion will be considered final.
  • Registration is strictly Online https://in.explara.com/beta/bbch15-race7 and no spot registration will be allowed
  • If you would like to get in touch with the the core team, please do shoot us an email at council@bbch.in
