Vitamin M

So the big question that some of you might be asking yourselves – who pays for these races?

 

Well the simple answer to that, is THE COMMUNITY.

 

Indeed, we have sponsors as you can see but these sponsors ONLY provide support in kind – prizes, food, water and medical support.

 

As you can imagine, this doesn’t cover the significant expenses involved in running a championship of this nature. Hence, the Vitamin M kitty. Basically, members of the biking community, riders, their friends and our well-wishers in general, contribute to the Vitamin M kitty in order to keep the BBCh healthy.

 

If you are racing, we would suggest a participation contribution of Rs.200. We do not believe in making this compulsory because in principle, we would never want a single person to be excluded on the offchance they are unable to cough up. If however, you would like to donate to the Vitamin M kitty, it would go a long way towards helping us keep up the level of organised bike racing in India.

 

You may choose to contribute on Race Day. Or, if you prefer, you can make a NEFT transfer too.

 

Please enter your NAME and EMAIL in the comments field while making an NEFT (if your bank allows this) so that we have details of folks who paid what. Useful as we maintain records for BBCh
